3d Annotation With Canvas

Overview

The 3d Annotation With Canvas holds a world-space canvas and Annotations that can be placed in the 3d scene. Normally you only need one canvas and after that you can add any number of 3d annotations. For more information, how you can use this annotations take a look at the Annotation Prefab
