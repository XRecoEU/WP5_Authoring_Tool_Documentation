Free Look Camera

Overview

The Free Look Camera can be controlled with the Mouse or with WASD keyboard buttons.

Inspector Properties

  • Cam: The camera that will be controlled by this component.
  • Controls: Specifies the input methods for controlling the camera. Options:
    • Mouse: Allows camera control using a mouse.
    • Touch: Enables touch gestures for camera control.
  • Speed: movement speed of the camera
  • Two Step Speed: second movement speed of the camera, enabled by left shift button
  • Invert Rotation: If checked invertes the rotation of the camera
  • Rotation Speed: speed of the camera rotation

How to Use

  1. Add the Free Look Camera GameObject to your scene.
  2. Assign a camera to the Cam property in the Inspector.
  3. Set a target object in the Target to Look At field. This is the object the camera will orbit around.
  4. Configure the initial distance, rotation, and zoom settings to suit your scene.
  5. Select the desired input controls (Mouse, Touch, or both).
  6. Activate the Camera view in the toolbar to place the camera on the right position.
