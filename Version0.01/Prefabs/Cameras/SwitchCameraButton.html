Switch Camera Button Prefab

Overview

The Switch Camera Button Prefab is designed to allow the user to switch between different cameras, providing a better overview of the scene. It creates a canvas with a 2D button that can be pressed in the final application.

Inspector Properties

  • In the Inspector of the OnClickReaction gameobject you can select the cameras you would like to toggle between.
  • Use the add button in the Objects To Activate-dropdown to select other gameobjects you would like to activate when you toggle between the camera view.
  • Use the add button in the Objects To Deactivate-dropdown to select other gameobjects you would like to deactivate when you toggle between the camera view.

How to Use

  1. Add the Switch Camera Button Prefab component to an empty GameObject in your scene. This Creates an 2D Button in the Play Mode.
  2. Add a camera that you like to switch between the main camera and the other view.
  3. Move the Camera to the right location, if not already visable activate the camera view in the lower toolbar.
  4. Drag and drop the Camera gameobject into the inspector of the OnClickReaction gameobject.
  5. There you can also activate and deactivate other gameobject.
  6. Use the Toggle button to change the camera view.

Editor Adjustments

To use the Switch Camera Button Prefab in your scene you can use the following adjustments in the editor:

  • Activate the camera view in the lower toolbar of the scene view to better position of the camera gameobject.
  • To move the camera use the three arrows for the three axes, for more informations look at the control options of the Orbit Camera.

Additional Instructions

To use the Switch Camera Button Prefab Prefab in your scene you can use the following adjustments:

  • You can change the text content of the button within the Text Component of the Button_Label gameobject.
