How to Use

  1. You can find all prefabs in the prefabs window under the XReco upper menu item. (picture of the Prefab Window)
  2. Have a look at the addiitional documentation of the explicit prefab.
  3. When you click on the create button in the prefabb window the prefab willl be added under the selected game object in the scene.
  4. Have a look in the inspector for additional options for your prefab.
