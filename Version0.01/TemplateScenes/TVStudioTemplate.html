TV Studio Template Scene

Overview

The TV Studio Template Scene is designed to assist users in creating their own TV studio filming experience. It offers a variety of features, such as the ability to insert 3D models, adjust camera perspectives for individual filming opportunities, and incorporate greenscreen videos within a 3D environment.

Contents

TV Studio Environment

The scene template creates a complete environment of a typical TV studio. It contains two stages, where the user can display different content. In the hierarchy, you will find the individual stages and their associated plugins, which allow you to add new content. For a better understanding, take a look at the Prefab Documentation

First Stage with Greenscreen video

On the first stage, you can insert a greenscreen, which will then be displayed on a stage with spotlights and two screens in the background. For detailed information on how to customize the greenscreen video, take a look the Greenscreen Video Prefab Documentation

Second Stage with an Exmaple Model

On the second stage, you can display various 3D models. To edit or modify this models, visit the Content Placement Helper Prefab Documentation To display the second stage in your application, you can place an Orbit Camera Prefab Documentation in the environment and use the Switch-Camera-Button to toggle between the different viewer's perspectives.

Recording Helper

With the Recording Helper Prefab, you can start and stop a recording directly in the play mode (inspector window). The recording will be captured based on the current camera view. You can save this recording as an MP4 file and use it for further purposes.

Setup Instructions

  1. Open the TV Studio Template Scene in Unity.
  2. Replace the example Greenscreen Video in the first stage with your own by dragging your video into the scene and aligning it as needed.
  3. Replace the example 3d model in the second stage with your own by dragging your model into the scene and aligning it as needed.
  4. Place a new camera in the scene and toggle between viewer's perspectives with help of the Switch Camera Button Prefab.
  5. Use the Recording Helper Prefab to create your own TV studio recording.

Customizing the Scene

  • Use the XReco Toolbar to change the Light Intensity.
  • Use other Prefabs to optimize your experience.

Best Practices

  • Use more cameras to create a better experience for the viewer.
  • Test the scene on all target platforms to ensure consistent performance and usability.
  • Have a look at other Scene Templates to get inspiration for more content.
