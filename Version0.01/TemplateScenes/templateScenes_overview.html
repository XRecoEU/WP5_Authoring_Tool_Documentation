Infographics Template Scene

The Infographics Template Scene is designed to help users create interactive and visually engaging 3D infographics for 2D screens, including mobile, PC and web platforms. This scene is pre-configured with essential components and serves as a starting point for your infographic projects.

TV Studio Template Scene

The TV Studio Template is designed to assist users in creating their own TV studio filming experience. It offers a variety of features, such as the ability to insert 3D models, adjust camera perspectives for individual filming opportunities, and incorporate greenscreen videos within a 3D environment.

How to Use

  1. Insert a new scene in Unity, click on the file header button and select new scene.
  2. Select the Scene Template you like to create.
